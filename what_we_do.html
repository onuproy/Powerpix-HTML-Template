what we do

PRINT

Graphics are at the heart and soul of every industry and even more so in the world of tools, parts, and hardware. The right graphics can show a person exactly what your product looks like, what it can do for them, and why your brand is the place to buy it from. The wrong graphics can leave a person confused, turned off, or uninterested. At PowerPix, we collaborate with brands to create visuals that help tell their story and sell their parts and products to the masses using cutting edge technology and an innovative eye for design.

You name the image type and we can create it for you. The automotive and electronics world is full of different visuals, from schematics to print advertisements, with each one requiring a different approach. PowerPix is your do-it-all graphics agency. Whether you need new images for an upcoming campaign, or you want help designing the instructional manual to a new product, we’re happy to lend a hand. We specialize in ads, flyers, banners, brochures, catalogs, drawings, and more. We also have the capabilities of creating 3D visuals as well. Whatever you need to depict, we’ll help you make it count.

packaging

It’s what inside that counts, but you first need to compel people to want to open the package in the first place. The right packaging can do wonders for your sales. The unboxing experiencing and packaging as a whole has gone through a bit of a revolution over the last 10 years or so, and it’s only going to continue in the future. Brands are learning that consumers of all ages enjoy packages that look appealing, and are a joy to open. For companies specializing in parts, components, and electronics, you’d be remiss not to invest in the best packaging experience possible. We’re happy to help.

We create custom packaging solutions that elevate your brand in the minds of consumers and help you stand out on retail shelves. We don’t use any boilerplate designs and everything is 100% custom, made from scratch just for you. Whether you have a design in mind and guidelines for us to work from or you want to start from the ground up, we can do it all. PowerPix produces labeling and packaging that sells for auto companies, parts, electronics, hardware, and more.
